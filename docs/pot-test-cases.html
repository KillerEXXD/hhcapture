⚠️ CRITICAL - Ante Handling
BB posts 25 ante (dead money) + 100 blind. The ante is NOT part of BB's live contribution. All players must match 100 (BB's blind), NOT 125. BB loses 125 total but only contributes 100 to the pot calculation.

⚠️ Multi-Street Pot Accumulation
Preflop pot = 900. On flop, Alice folds after checking. Bob bets 500, Charlie calls 500. Alice's 300 preflop contribution stays in pot as dead money. Only Bob and Charlie can win the full 1,900 pot.
